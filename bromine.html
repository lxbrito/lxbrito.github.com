Bromine Elements

Inspired by Gold-Elements, I decided to build a set of inputs with built-in validation for some brazilian specific needs. Starting with CEP (zip code), a simple and straightforward customization of the gold-zip-input to the brazilian format, I advanced to more complex formats and validations such as CPF and CNPJ.

Currently the set also includes a NIT/PIS/PASEP input and I'm refining a dirty-implemented money-input, from which I expect to extract a formatted-number-input with user-defined separators and delimiters.
